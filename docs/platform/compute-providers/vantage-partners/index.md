# Vantage Partners

Vantage-partnered providers offer pre-configured, managed compute environments that integrate directly with the Vantage platform.

## Partners

- **[atNorth](/platform/compute-providers/vantage-partners/atnorth)** — Sustainable HPC in Nordic data centers, powered by 100% renewable energy.
- **[BuzzHPC](/platform/compute-providers/vantage-partners/buzz-hpc)** — Specialized HPC infrastructure and expert support for demanding workloads.
- **[Responsible Compute](/platform/compute-providers/vantage-partners/responsible-compute)** — Ethically and environmentally focused compute for sustainability-conscious teams.

Email [support@vantagecompute.ai](mailto:support@vantagecompute.ai) to connect a partner provider to your workspace.
