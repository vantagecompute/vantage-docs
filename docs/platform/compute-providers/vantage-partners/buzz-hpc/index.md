# BuzzHPC

BuzzHPC provides purpose-built HPC infrastructure and expert support for compute-intensive workloads.

Use BuzzHPC when your workload needs hardware tuned specifically for HPC — specialized interconnects, custom node configurations, or expert optimization for CFD, FEA, and other simulation-heavy workflows.

:::tip
BuzzHPC is a managed service — you pay a premium over raw cloud for the specialized hardware and support. Compare against self-managed cloud clusters if your workload doesn't require custom interconnects or expert tuning.
:::
