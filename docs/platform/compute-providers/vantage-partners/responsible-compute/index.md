# Responsible Compute

Responsible Compute provides HPC infrastructure with a focus on environmental and social responsibility.

Use Responsible Compute when your organization has sustainability or ESG commitments that extend to your compute choices.
The partnership includes carbon reporting, ethical AI frameworks, and compute resources for nonprofit and research organizations.

:::tip
Responsible Compute may offer preferential pricing for nonprofit and academic organizations. Ask your Vantage account team about eligibility when evaluating cost.
:::
