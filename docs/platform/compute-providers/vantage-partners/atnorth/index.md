# atNorth

atNorth provides sustainable, high-performance compute in Iceland and Sweden, running on 100% renewable energy.

Use atNorth when environmental impact matters alongside performance — AI training, scientific computing, and large-scale HPC jobs that need serious infrastructure without the carbon footprint of traditional cloud.

Natural cooling and renewable power sources keep operational costs predictable.

:::tip
atNorth's renewable energy model can make large training runs more cost-stable than public cloud — renewable power rates are less volatile than on-demand pricing in high-demand regions.
:::
