# Compute Providers

Compute Providers is where you connect external compute resources so Vantage can provision clusters on your behalf.

[Get started](/platform/compute-providers/get-started) · [Concepts](/platform/compute-providers/concepts)

## What you'll find inside

- **[Public clouds](/platform/compute-providers/public-clouds)** — AWS, Azure, GCP, and Cudo Compute accounts. Elastic capacity, spot pricing, global regions.
- **[On-premises](/platform/compute-providers/on-premises)** — Your own hardware or private cloud, connected via LXD or direct on-prem integration.
- **[Vantage Partners](/platform/compute-providers/vantage-partners)** — Pre-integrated managed providers: atNorth, BuzzHPC, and Responsible Compute.
