# Google Cloud Platform

Connect your GCP project so Vantage can provision Compute Engine clusters in your chosen region.

## Credentials

Vantage needs a **Service Account** with IAM permissions to manage Compute Engine resources in your project.

## Steps

1. Go to **Admin** > **Cloud Accounts**.
1. Click <kbd>Create Cloud Account</kbd> and select **GCP**.
1. Enter an **Account name** and optional description.
1. Expand the **Advanced** section and provide your service account credentials.
1. Submit. Vantage validates and saves the account.

:::tip

Sustained use discounts apply automatically for VMs running continuously throughout the month — no reservations needed.
:::
