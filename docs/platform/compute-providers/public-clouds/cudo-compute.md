# Cudo Compute

Connect your Cudo Compute account so Vantage can provision GPU clusters at competitive cloud prices.

Choose Cudo Compute for on-demand or reserved GPU capacity at lower cost than AWS or GCP — especially for AI training, inference, and rendering workloads.

## Credentials

Vantage needs a **Datacenter ID** and an **API key** from your Cudo Compute account.

## Steps

1. Go to **Admin** > **Cloud Accounts**.
1. Click <kbd>Create Cloud Account</kbd> and select **Cudo Compute**.
1. Enter an **Account name** and optional description.
1. Enter your **Datacenter ID** and **API key**.
1. Submit. Vantage validates and saves the account.

:::tip
Cudo Compute's competitive GPU pricing makes it worth evaluating alongside AWS and GCP for sustained AI training workloads. Compare on-demand rates before choosing reserved capacity.
:::
