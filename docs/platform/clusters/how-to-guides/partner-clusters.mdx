# Partner clusters

Vantage partners give you access to specialized HPC infrastructure without managing cloud accounts yourself.
The wizard flow is the same as public cloud clusters — the difference is the cloud account type.

## Prerequisites

A partner cloud account configured in Vantage — click the **Settings** gear icon (bottom-left of the sidebar), then select **Cloud Accounts**.
Contact the partner or your Vantage account team to get the credentials needed for account setup.

## Create a cluster

1. **Open Clusters** — Click **Clusters** in the left sidebar, then click <kbd>Prepare Cluster</kbd>.

1. **Choose type** — Select **Slurm** or **Kubernetes**, then click **Continue**.

1. **Configure the cluster:**
   - Enter a **Cluster Name** (max 27 characters).
   - Select your **partner cloud account**. The provider is detected automatically.

1. **Submit** — Click <kbd>Prepare Cluster</kbd>. The cluster connects once the partner infrastructure is provisioned.

## Supported partners

| Partner | Specialization |
|---|---|
| atNorth | Sustainable HPC — geothermal and hydroelectric-powered data centers in Iceland and Sweden |
| BuzzHPC | High-performance bare-metal and GPU infrastructure |
| Cudo Compute | Distributed computing marketplace — access underutilized compute globally |
| Responsible Compute | Green computing with a focus on renewable energy sourcing |

:::info
Partner cluster availability and supported regions depend on your agreement with the partner.
Contact your Vantage account team if a partner option is not visible in the cloud account selector.
:::
