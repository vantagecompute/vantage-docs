# Teams — How-to guides

## Manage team membership

| Task | How |
|---|---|
| Add members | Open the team detail page, click **Add Members**, enter email addresses |
| Change a member's role | Open the team detail page, click the member's current role and select a new one |
| Remove a member | Open the team detail page, click the member's action menu and select **Remove** |
| Leave a team | Open the team detail page and click **Leave Team** (only if you're a member, not the last admin) |

## Manage team resources

| Task | How |
|---|---|
| Grant cluster access | Open the team detail page, go to **Cluster Access**, click **Add Cluster** |
| Remove cluster access | Open the team detail page, go to **Cluster Access**, click the cluster's action menu and select **Remove** |
| View team resources | Resources are visible on the team detail page under each resource tab (clusters, jobs, storage) |
| Transfer resource ownership | Contact your organization admin — resource ownership transfer requires admin-level permissions |

## Manage team settings

| Task | How |
|---|---|
| Rename a team | Open the team detail page, click the team name and edit |
| Delete a team | Open the team settings, scroll to **Danger Zone**, click **Delete Team**. Resources owned by the team must be transferred or deleted first |
