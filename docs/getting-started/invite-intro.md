Invite your colleagues, manage permissions, and assign team membership in your Vantage organization.

## 1. Navigate to the Users section

Select the [Users](https://app.vantagecompute.ai/admin/users) option from the settings dropdown to navigate to the user management dashboard.

![invite-intro-00](./img/invite-intro-00.png)

## 2. Click "New Invite"

Find the "New Invite" button in the upper right-hand corner of the Users tab. Click the button to open the new invite form.

![invite-intro-01](./img/invite-intro-01.png)

## 3. Enter Invitee Email Address and Assign Permissions

Complete the "New Invite" form by entering the invitee's email address and desired permissions group(s).

Then click the "Send Invite Request" button to initiate the invitation process.

![invite-intro-02](./img/invite-intro-02.png)

Permission groups determine what actions a user can perform within your organization, such as managing clusters or submitting jobs.

## 4. Invitation Summary

The invitee will receive an email from `invites@vantagecompute.ai`. They should click the link in the email to join the Vantage organization.

Once accepted, the user will appear in the Users list with the assigned permissions.
